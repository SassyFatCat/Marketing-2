What's The Point?

Our Mission

The mission of Use My Tech Stuff™ is to connect people with the technology that they need.

Skip the Middleman

Retail stores charge excessive prices for technology, often with very limited return policies. Skip the middleman and get your technology straight from people just like you.

Rent For
Less

Whether its for an emergency, business or personal use, we get the technology you need in your hands at a fraction of the cost. When you're done, you simply return it.

How Does It Work?

Renters create a profile and post pieces of technology that they would like to rent out.

Renters can browse pieces of technology by category, distance, and price and communicate directly with renters about their postings.

With multiple payment options, pick-up scheduling and a verified review system, you'll never use anything else!
